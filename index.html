European Cultural Mosaic

25 iterations exploring the architecture, cuisine, art, and lifestyle of Europe's most iconic nations. From Swiss minimalism to Art Nouveau extravagance — a journey through design, converging toward one final vision.

6 countries 25 iterations Pure HTML & CSS

Phase 1 · v1–v8

Wide Exploration

Radically different visual directions. Each version explores a distinct mood, layout, and typographic voice for presenting European culture.

v1

Swiss Minimalism

Clean grid layout, Helvetica type, muted palette with a single red accent. Inspired by the International Typographic Style.

minimalist grid swiss typography
v2

Vintage Travel Poster

Bold saturated colors, retro serif typography, poster-style country blocks. Feels like a 1960s travel agency window.

retro bold-color poster serif
v3

Editorial Magazine

Luxury editorial layout with serif headings, multi-column text, and a black-white-gold palette. A grand cultural tour in print form.

editorial luxury serif multi-column
v4

Brutalist

Raw monospace typography, harsh black and white with neon green accents. Deliberately confrontational, intentionally rough.

brutalist monospace raw high-contrast
v5

Art Nouveau

Ornamental borders, gold and forest green palette, flowing organic shapes. Inspired by the decorative arts movement of 1890s Europe.

ornamental art-nouveau gold organic
v6

Modern Dark Mode

Dark interface with neon gradients and glassmorphism cards. A sleek, contemporary take on presenting European nightlife and modern cities.

dark-mode neon glassmorphism modern
v7

Watercolor Artistic

Soft pastel washes, rounded shapes, and a gentle hand-drawn feel. Evokes the charm of European countryside watercolor paintings.

watercolor pastel artistic soft
v8

Map-Centric

Spatial layout centered around a visual map of Europe. Countries as positioned elements with coordinate-style data presentation.

map interactive spatial data-viz
